Our Mission

To provide for Ethiopians' living in Japan including students with knowledge and information to integrate into Japanese society and become independent.

Our Service

We provide for Ethiopians' living in Japan including students with knowledge and information to integrate into Japanese society and become independent.

We also give supports on their daily live activities and job hunting.

Further more, we aim to deepen international exchange and cooperation between Japan and Ethiopia by conducting activities related to Ethiopia's educational support and environmental education for the general public.

Specific Services

We will carry out the following businesses related to specified non-profit activities in order to achieve the purposes.

  1. Network building business for Ethiopian residents in Japan
  2. Human rights, living and employment support projects for Ethiopian residents in Japan
  3. Cultural and sports exchange program between Ethiopia and Japan
  4. Human rights and poverty support projects in Ethiopia
  5. Company introduction business in Japan and Ethiopia
  6. Seminar business on professional development
  7. Education support project
  8. Environmental education business by holding lectures and tours in the community and schools
  9. Other businesses necessary to achieve the purpose
